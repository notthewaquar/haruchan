The Broken Weapons, in particular, are almost entirely tied to the ICT Trader NPC, who was added within the Between Dimensions DLC.

The only items here that are individual to specific Dungeons are the different Jelly-types, as each Dungeon - apart from the Wanderer Dungeon - has its own unique Jelly-type that appears within it.
